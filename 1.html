About Me


Meet Maya, a recent graduate bursting with enthusiasm for web development. With a degree in computer science under her belt, she’s excited to dive into the tech world. Maya spent her university years honing her skills, mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through hands-on projects and coding bootcamps.

As a fresher, Maya is eager to learn and adapt. She spends her evenings exploring the latest frameworks, like React and Vue.js, often experimenting with side projects that reflect her growing expertise. Her portfolio, though small, showcases a vibrant personal website, a few interactive web apps, and contributions to open-source projects.

Maya believes in the power of community, regularly attending local meetups and hackathons. She loves connecting with seasoned developers, soaking in their insights, and sharing her fresh perspective. Though she knows she has much to learn, Maya is confident that her passion and determination will guide her on her journey to becoming a skilled web developer. With every line of code, she feels closer to her dream of creating impactful, user-friendly web experiences.
